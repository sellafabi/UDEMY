The Good Bad Mother

Young Soon is a single mother and pig farmer who raised her son Kang Ho alone.
Despite her love for him, her strict parenting caused Kang Ho to view her as a bad
mother. As an adult, Kang Ho became a cold-hearted prosecutor and kept his distance
from his mother. However, an unexpected accident caused him to return to his hometown
and start over with Young Soon. Kang Ho's childhood friend Mi Joo, known for her warm
heart and strong sense of justice, also reconnected with him after his accident, leading
to a transformative experience for her.
